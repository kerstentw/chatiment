Backtest your Trading Group


UPDATED: 4 March 2019 - Welcome to Chatiment Alpha v0.1

Chatiment is a Trading tool for backtesting the wisdom of the crowd. I started this app when I was perusing a crypto-currency trading group, and I realized that the process of even getting cursory validation of the assertions and ideas in the chat was an obnoxious process. In its final form, Chatiment will allow a deep analysis, filtering, and message aggregation. For now, though it just matches groups of messages to some aggregated prices. I will be adding more apps such as Line, Discord, Slack, etc. as I have time.

Unfortunately, I have a day job and only can really work on this site on weekends. I will attempt to make updates each Saturday as this has already been a pretty fun project to mess around with.

If you have any ideas or suggestions, feel free to send me an email at hello.chatiment@gmail.com. I will be setting up the email domain in a bit but for now this is it.

Kakao Talk

Kakao Talk is one of the most popular chat apps in South Korea, one of the biggest markets for crypto.

1. Go inside to your chatroom which you intended to export the chat log.

2. Press the menu button of your Samsung device.

3. Then 3 options will show up at the bottom of the screen. Invite, Leave and More

4. Tap on “More“

5. It will lead you to the Chat room setting where you find the “Export”

6. 3 choices you can choose. Send only text , send all messages or save in SD card.

7. Normally you can choose ” Send Text Only”

8. It will compile the .Txt file and later prompt you where you want to send/share the .txt file. Either to your email, to your Whatsapp and etc.
